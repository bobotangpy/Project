Behind The Voices

Eric Edelstein (Grizzly)


Eric Edelstein is an American actor, voice actor, and writer, born on April 23, 1977 in Patuxent River, Maryland. Eric's more popular acting roles include the characters Spitter from the 2007 horror-thriller film The Hills Have Eyes II and ACO Max in the 2009 comedy film Hotel for Dogs. One of the more recent films he did was the 2015 action film Jurassic World. He's voice acted other characters from two other cartoons, which are the characters Chad from Cartoon Network's Clarence and B.O.B from Nickelodeon's Monsters vs. Aliens.

Bobby Moynihan (Panda)


Robert Michael "Bobby" Moynihan Jr. is an American actor, voice actor, and comedian. Bobby is best known for his sketch performances and reoccurring characters on Saturday Night Live, which kick-started his professional career. Bobby has since moved on to achieve several minor roles in films and other media such as Monsters University, Comedy Bang! Bang!, Annie, and Inside Out. He also plays Mel, a pug, in The Secret Life of Pets, Hal the Dog from P.B.S. Kids‘ Nature Cat and Mayor Muldoon in The Nut Job 2: Nutty by Nature.

Demetri Martin (Ice Bear)


Demetri Evan Martin is an American actor, comedian, voice actor, and an artist. Demetri Martin was born on May 25, 1973 in New York City, New York. He attended NYU School Of Law after receiving a full scholarship, but dropped out due to boredom and became a stand-up comedian. On June 1, 2012, Martin married long-time girlfriend Rachael Beame in Santa Monica, California.

Charlyne Yi (Chloe)


Charlyne Amanda Yi is an American actress, comedian, musician, and writer. She was born in Los Angeles, California. She is the daughter of Lydia and Luciano Yi. Her mother, a native of the Philippines, is of Philippine and Spanish descent, and her father is of Mexican, Korean, Irish, German, French, and American ancestry. She attended the University of California, Riverside before leaving to pursue a full-time career in comedy.
